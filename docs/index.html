Our Mission

Business Development to shape our future.

Improve Relationships

To improve the state of African & African diasporan relations by engaging in business, political and international affairs to foster economic development across Trans-Atlantic populations. The purpose of the forum is to encourage today‚Äôs leaders of society to engage in entrepreneurship and shape regional agendas.

To Serve

To service the African & African diasporan state of the world through public and private cooperation. The goal of the forum is to sustain strong business relationships through integrated concepts of public business meetings, research networks and cross-regional collaboration.

To Deliver Value

To deliver values to its partners, affiliates and attendees who are strongly committed to global, regional and Pan-African issues. The African Global Atlantic and Indian Ocean Economic Forum was founded by Black West Africa WallStreet Nigeria, LTD in 2016, the forum is tied to no political, partisan or national interests.

Our Vision

A global agenda from a global perspective.

Promote Investments

To promote integration, regional and global investments between Africa and the African-Diaspora by encouraging collaboration between Black-Owned Businesses, Banks, Institutions, Professional groups and associations in collaborative efforts to shape global agendas.
